About HomeHVACFilters

Who We Are

HomeHVACFilters.com is an independent review and education site focused on one thing: helping homeowners choose the right HVAC filter. We research, compare, and test furnace filters so you don't have to spend hours figuring out the difference between MERV 8 and MERV 13 (though we have a whole guide on that if you're curious).

We're not affiliated with any filter manufacturer. Our recommendations are based on performance, value, and real-world usability — not sponsorship deals.

Our Editorial Process

Every guide on this site follows the same process:

  1. Research — We review manufacturer specifications, ASHRAE testing data, and user feedback across multiple retailers.
  2. Compare — We evaluate filters on MERV rating accuracy, airflow characteristics, size availability, and cost per month of use.
  3. Recommend — We pick the best options for different needs (budget, allergies, pets, etc.) and explain why.
  4. Update — We revisit our guides regularly to reflect new products, price changes, and reader feedback.

We aim to be genuinely helpful. That means sometimes recommending a $5 filter over a $25 one, and being honest when a more expensive option isn't worth the premium.
